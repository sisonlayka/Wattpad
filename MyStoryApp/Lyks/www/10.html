True Love Waits

Her girlfriend doesn't talk about it 'cause she doesn't want her to be depressed or worry about her condition.

The lesbian smiled and said, "I really love you very much to the point that I can't live without you"

Her girlfriend asked again, "Then what if one day, Im going to leave you? "

She answered, "If you're going to leave me, don't say goodbye"

Her girlfriend looked at her eyes and asked, "Why?"

She hugged her so tight and said ...

To bear in my mind that I'll be still here waiting for you even though I already know the fact that you're not going to come back anymore ..
